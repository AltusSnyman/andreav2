## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
1.  **Best time to lay new turf?** September (cooler air/sunshine) is ideal. November becomes too warm.
2.  **Why hire a landscaper?** Professional design saves money long-term, ensures aesthetics match vision, and correct plant selection.
3.  **How to keep garden healthy?** Examine plants before buying, use composted waste, watch for bugs, clean in fall, fertilise correctly, weed regularly, water deeply once a week (2 inches).
4.  **Benefits of landscaping?** Improves quality of life, energy efficiency (shade), reduces erosion/runoff, noise reduction, habitat for wildlife.
5.  **What is included in maintenance?** Mowing, pest control, spring clean up, trimming, fertilization, weed control, mulching, pruning, leaf removal.
