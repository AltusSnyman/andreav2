# Consultation, Residential Landscaping, Darwin, NT

When it comes to designing the perfect landscape, having a team of professionals like ours at Darwin Landscapers can help make the job easier from start to finish.  Our landscaping design specialists work with you to create a customised plan that fits your design ideas.  Whether we’re working on a brand new garden, or sprucing up your existing one, consultations are an integral part of our process.  We work with clients for residential and commercial landscaping alike, creating softscapes and hardscapes that are [designed](http://darwinlandscapers.com/garden-design.html) to enhance your property.  With our team, you’ll see your custom landscaping come to life just as you envisioned it.

**Landscape Design**  
In garden and landscape design, we believe that consulting with our clients is one of the most important parts of our process of creating the perfect design.  We create custom landscapes that are reflections of our client’s vision, integrating everything from plants and trees to the lawn and hardscape.  In addition to the visual aspects of the landscape, we also perform a full property assessment that allows us to better understand the terrain in which we’re working.  By integrating things like the type of soil, irrigation and sunlight into your landscape design, we help you property look its best year round.

As a professional landscaping company, we’re happy to be able to offer our customers all of the landscaping services that they need.  We offer both softscape and hardscape designs, allowing our clients to integrate their landscaping to fit together as a seamless design.  Ensuring that the hardscape components of your location blend in with your garden and other foliage, our final products have a complete and finished look that enhances your property as a whole.  Consulting with clients to find the items that best match their design ideas, our hardscape can be used for residential and commercial properties alike.
