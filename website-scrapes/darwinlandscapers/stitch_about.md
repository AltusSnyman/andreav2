## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the best time of year to lay new turf?**
A: September is ideal due to cooler air and sunshine. November can be too warm for new turf to establish easily.

**Q: How much does landscaping cost?**
A: A simple residential garden (mostly plants) costs around **$150/m²**. A higher-end garden with hardscaping (paving, walls) costs around **$300/m²**.

**Q: Do you do commercial landscaping?**
A: Yes! We work with businesses to create professional, impressive landscapes that reflect well on your company.
