# Our Services

At Darwin Landscapers, we offer a comprehensive range of landscaping services for both residential and commercial properties.

## 1. Garden Design & Consultation
Whether you are starting from scratch or renovating an existing garden, our design team works with you to bring your vision to life.
-   **Consultation**: We start with a personalized consultation to understand your goals, assess your property (soil, sunlight, irrigation), and discuss your budget.
-   **Custom Design**: We select plants, trees, and flowers that thrive in the Darwin climate and design layouts that are both beautiful and functional.
-   **Service Areas**: Residential and Commercial design.

## 2. Turf Laying & Lawn Care
Nothing beats a lush green lawn. We handle the entire process from soil preparation to installation.
-   **Turf Installation**: We measure, prep the soil (removing old lawn/weeds), level the ground, and lay high-quality turf.
-   **Lawn Maintenance**: Regular mowing, watering advice, and dry season preparation.
-   **Pricing**: Turf installation generally ranges from **$18 to $34 per square metre**, with an average starting around **$28/m²**.

## 3. Landscape Construction (Hardscaping)
We build the structural elements that define your outdoor space, blending them seamlessly with your softscapes.
-   **Paving**: Driveways, paths, and pool surrounds.
    -   *Estimate*: **~$90/m²** for plain concrete to **~$500/m²** for premium finishes.
-   **Retaining Walls**: Concrete, timber, or dry stone walls to manage soil levels and create garden beds.
-   **Decking**: Custom garden decking.
    -   *Estimate*: **~$300 - $400/m²**.

## 4. Irrigation Systems
Essential for the Darwin climate, we design and install systems to keep your garden thriving while saving water.
-   **Installation**: Custom systems (sprinklers, controllers, timers) tailored to your property size and plant needs.
    -   *Front Garden Estimate*: **~$2100+**
    -   *Rear Garden Estimate*: **~$1300+**
-   **Maintenance & Repair**: Fixes for pipes, fittings, and controllers.
    -   *Rate*: **~$90** for the first hour, **$25/hour** thereafter.

## 5. Garden Maintenance
Keep your property looking its best year-round with our scheduled maintenance services.
-   **Services**: Weeding, pruning, trimming, mulching, pest control, fertilising, and leaf removal.
-   **Yard Clean Ups**: End-of-lease cleans and general tidy-ups.
-   **Schedule**: Weekly, monthly, or seasonal visits available.

## 6. Tree Services
-   **Tree Removal & Trimming**: Expert care for trees to ensure safety and aesthetics, preventing issues with foundations or shade.
